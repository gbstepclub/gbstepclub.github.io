Contact Us

Please drop by the Club, phone us, or send us a letter. We are always interested in hearing from twelve-step groups that want to use the Club's facility to start new meetings. The Club is available for any approved twelve-step recovery group wanting to hold meetings and support people seeking recovery through such groups.

Driving instructions from I-495 (Capital Beltway):
Exit north onto Kenilworth Avenue (MD 201), which becomes Edmondston Road north of I-495. Turn right onto Crescent Road and drive about 1 mile to a four-way stop with a Sunoco service station on the right-hand side. Turn right at the four-stay stop. The Club is in the basement of the building on the left hand side of the street – under the green awning. Park behind the building, next to the COOP grocery store. Parking is free.

Driving instructions from MD-295 (Baltimore-Washington Parkway):
Take the exit toward MD-193/Greenbelt/NASA Goddard. Turn right onto Southway and then turn right onto Crescent Road. Drive to the four-way stop with a Sunoco service station on the left-hand side. Turn left at the four-stay stop. The Club is in the basement of the building on the left hand side of the street – under the green awning. Park behind the building, next to the COOP grocery store. Parking is free.
