Club Finances

The By-Laws mandate the Board to maintain a prudent reserve equal to three months of operating expenses based on the average monthly operating expense of the previous six months. As stated in the By-Laws, expenses will be met by the following means:
  • Rents from groups that meet at the Club.
  • Fees from special activities sponsored by the Club, such as dances, dinners, retreats, picnics, and other special events approved by the Board of Directors.
  • Contributions from twelve-step groups and individuals who meet at the Club.
  • Contributions of materials to help the Club, provided that the contributions remain anonymous.
The Club Treasurer is authorized to use funds necessary to purchase supplies, equipment, or merchandise authorized by the Board. The By-Laws state that neither the Board nor any officer or paid servant of the Club will have the authority to borrow any monies, obligate any Club funds, or create any lien against any Club property unless authorized to do so by a majority of the Club members attending a duly called meeting of the membership. As indicated, the By-Laws mandate the Board to establish and maintain a prudent reserve.
