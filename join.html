To Join the Club

Anyone who participates in a twelve-step program represented at the Club is welcome to join the Club. Membership is not required to attend meetings or to participate in social activities at the Club but it entitles a person to vote in Club elections and to have a say-so in policy matters affecting the Club.

The membership dues are $5.00 per month, or $50.00 per year, as they have been since the Club opened.

Contact us if you are interested in becoming a member.
